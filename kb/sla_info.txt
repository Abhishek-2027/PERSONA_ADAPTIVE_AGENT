Service Level Agreement (SLA)

Overview:
Our SLA defines uptime guarantees and service commitments.

Uptime Guarantee:
- 99.5% uptime for Basic and Pro plans.
- 99.9% uptime for Enterprise plan.

Service Credits:
- If uptime drops below SLA threshold, eligible customers receive service credits.
- Credit percentage depends on outage duration.

Response Time:
- Standard support: 24-hour response.
- Priority support: 4-hour response.
- Enterprise support: 1-hour response.

Incident Escalation:
- Tier 1: Support engineer.
- Tier 2: Senior engineer.
- Tier 3: Infrastructure team.

Compliance:
- GDPR compliant.
- SOC 2 certified.
- Data encryption at rest and in transit.

For contractual SLA adjustments, contact the enterprise sales team.
