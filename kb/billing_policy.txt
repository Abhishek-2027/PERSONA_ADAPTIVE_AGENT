Billing and Subscription Policy

Overview:
Our billing operates on a monthly subscription basis.

Subscription Plans:
- Basic Plan: $49/month
- Pro Plan: $199/month
- Enterprise Plan: Custom pricing

Billing Cycle:
- Billing occurs on the same calendar date each month.
- Invoices are generated automatically and sent via email.

Refund Policy:
- Refunds are available within 7 days of initial subscription.
- No refunds for partial monthly usage.

Payment Methods:
- Credit/Debit Card
- Bank Transfer (Enterprise only)

Invoice Access:
- Users can download invoices from the billing dashboard.
- Past invoices are stored for 12 months.

Failed Payment:
- Account is marked overdue after 3 days.
- Service suspension after 7 days of non-payment.

For enterprise contract or SLA-related billing inquiries, contact the account manager.
