Login and Account Access Troubleshooting

Common Login Issues:

1. Incorrect Password
Solution:
- Use the "Forgot Password" option.
- Reset password via email verification link.
- Ensure password meets complexity requirements.

2. Account Locked
Cause:
- Multiple failed login attempts.

Solution:
- Wait 15 minutes before retrying.
- Contact support if locked for more than 24 hours.

3. Two-Factor Authentication Failure
Solution:
- Verify time synchronization on device.
- Use backup authentication codes.
- Re-register authenticator app if necessary.

4. Email Not Verified
Solution:
- Check inbox and spam folder.
- Request new verification email.

Security Best Practices:
- Enable multi-factor authentication.
- Use strong and unique passwords.
- Do not share credentials with others.
